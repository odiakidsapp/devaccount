About Me

Elementary school teacher turned Android developer, creating educational technology that bridges classroom experience with innovative app development for regional languages.

Hello! I'm Abinash Mahapatra, an Android developer and elementary school teacher in government schools of Odisha. My dual experience as an educator and developer gives me a unique perspective on creating educational applications that truly serve children's learning needs.

Working directly with students in Odisha's government schools, I understand firsthand the challenges children face when quality educational content isn't available in their native language. This real-world teaching experience drives my passion for developing apps that bridge the gap between technology and traditional education.

With expertise in both classroom teaching and Android development, I focus on building intuitive, engaging, and culturally relevant applications. My current mission is developing educational apps for regional Indian languages, starting with Odia, ensuring children can learn and grow in their mother tongue while having access to modern educational tools.

I believe that technology should serve as a bridge to knowledge, not a barrier. Through my combined experience as a teacher and developer, I create apps that not only educate but also celebrate local languages and cultures, making quality education accessible to every child.
